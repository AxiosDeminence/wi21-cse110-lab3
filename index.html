Some stuff I found interesting when learning html/css

Before I learned html/css, I often browsed and participated in forums that used bbcode. Many of the forums that I go to now has their software favoring wysiwg editors over BBcode. So some of the stuff I learned was new and some of the stuff was old. For example:

  • Bold elements have two tag options (<b>content</b> and <strong>content</strong>).
    BBCode had only one option: [b]content[/b].

  • Italicized elements have two tag options as well! (<i>content</i> and <em>content</em>). BBCode once again only had one options: [i]content[/i].

  • Line breaks in BBCode were generally handled as is. You broke a line and it showed up in the post. Usually it got translated to a bunch of <p> tags for the browser to parse.
